Simplified economy

Improved Anvil is a mod that is usually enjoyed by players who have beaten the game many times. Every run is ridden with the same routines: picking up lots of loot after each battle, selling it to the vendors, making sure you have enough gems to craft a certain item. Eventually, this gets old. People who still play IA want to focus on combat. By numerous requests, we came up with the component that helps the players get rid of the boring stuff.

The idea is simple: the majority of loot found in containers and carried by enemies is replaced with its selling price in gold. By rough estimation, the amount of trips to the vendors should decrease by 70%. Don't worry, all the quest items, unique and rare pieces of equipment, scrolls, potions and ammunition are still there for you to find.

The lists of items were made semi-automatically. We've checked them carefully afterwards, but we're not perfect. If something is not where you expect it to be, let us know and we'll fix it.

As an added bonus, the craftsmen (Cromwell and Cespenar) don't require generic gems and trinkets to craft certain item recipes. The items section of the readme contains more information regarding the specifics.

Thanks to Bulian for pushing me to make this thing happen and helping to verify the lists and make sure we did not overlook something important.
